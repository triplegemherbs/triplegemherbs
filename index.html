At Triple Gem Herbs, I approach herbalism through the dual lenses of traditional knowledge and scientific inquiry, because I believe there is true medicine at that intersection.

Through herbal consultations and botanical education, I support two kinds of people: those who want guidance supporting their health with herbs, and those who want to learn to develop their own herbal practice. Often, they're the same person.

My work is rooted in reverence for nature and herbal traditions, and I acknowledge the profound healing available to those who seek connection with plants.

About Jasmine

Jasmine Zenderland founded Triple Gem Herbs out of a deep belief that plants and people belong in relationship with each other. She holds a B.S. in Botany and an M.A. in Ecology & Evolutionary Biology, and has studied traditional herbalism and ethnobotany for over a decade, bringing a unique combination of scientific rigor and traditional knowledge to her practice.

Jasmine offers 1:1 herbal consultations for those navigating specific health concerns or seeking to experience their most vibrant health through herbs. She also offers botanical education for those who want to learn to use plants themselves and develop relationships with these beings.

Jasmine teaches yoga, is an instructor in the sustainability department at an online university, and works in restoration ecology — a background that shapes how she sees the world of plants, fungi, and healing. When she's not at her desk, you'll find her working in the garden, practicing yoga, or hunting mushrooms on the far northern coast of California.
