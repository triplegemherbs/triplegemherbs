About Jasmine

Jasmine Zenderland is a botanist, educator, and herbalist who brings together scientific rigor and traditional plant knowledge to support holistic understanding and personal empowerment. With deep roots in both academic research and community-based herbalism, Jasmine bridges worlds to make plant wisdom accessible, meaningful, and transformative.

Through Triple Gem Herbs, she offers one-on-one herbal consulting, plant walks, immersive workshops, and educational programs that honor both evidence-based approaches and ancestral traditions. Her work is rooted in connection — to plants, to people, and to place — and guided by curiosity, integrity, and care.

Whether you're seeking personal guidance, deepening your herbal knowledge, or cultivating a stronger relationship with the natural world, Jasmine creates a space where science meets tradition — and where healing, learning, and growth can unfold.
