How It Works

What a Consultation Is Like

Sessions are conversational and centered on you. I take notes throughout, document key plant recommendations, and send a follow-up summary so nothing gets lost.

We explore your health concerns and current context together — understanding your body's patterns, what's been tried before, and what feels right to work toward.

Sessions typically include:

  • A discussion of your concerns, symptoms, or goals
  • Exploration of body systems and underlying patterns
  • Herbal recommendations — teas, tinctures, or capsules
  • Food-as-medicine considerations
  • Lifestyle suggestions: movement, rituals, sleep, seasonal rhythms
  • Educational resources and Q&A time

What You May Receive

  • Personalized herbal recommendations
  • Plant education and preparation guidance
  • Seasonal and lifestyle suggestions
  • Written follow-up notes
  • Pathways to deepen your own herbal practice

Accessibility

Sliding Scale & Accessibility

I offer a limited number of sliding scale spots each month for people who need them. Clients are trusted to choose the rate that is appropriate for their situation — no documentation required.

If cost is a barrier, please mention it when you reach out. I want herbalism to be accessible.

A Note on What I Provide

Herbal consultations are educational and supportive in nature. I do not diagnose or treat disease, and my services are not a substitute for medical care. I do not make prescription recommendations. Please continue working with your healthcare providers.
